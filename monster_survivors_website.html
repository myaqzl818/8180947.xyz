üéØ How to Play Monster Survivors

Getting started with Monster Survivors is simple, but mastering the game requires skill and strategy. Use your mouse or touch controls to navigate your character and aim your attacks. The goal is to survive each wave of monsters while collecting resources and power-ups that appear throughout the battlefield.

Pay attention to different monster types - each has unique behaviors and weaknesses. Some monsters are fast but weak, while others are slow but deal massive damage. Learning these patterns is key to developing effective survival strategies.

Time management is crucial in Monster Survivors. Between waves, you have limited time to position yourself strategically and prepare for the next assault. Use this time wisely to maximize your chances of survival.
